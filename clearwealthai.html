Why You Should Start Investing with Just €50 — Even Today

By ClearWealth AI · 5 min read · April 2026

Most people think investing is for the wealthy. They picture Wall Street traders and six-figure starting capital. The truth? The best time to start is right now, even if you only have €50.

"The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient." — Warren Buffett

What €50 per month actually becomes

If you invest €50 per month starting at age 25, with a historical average return of 7% per year, here is what happens:

AgeYears investedTotal paid inPortfolio value
3510 years€6,000€8,654
4520 years€12,000€26,707
5530 years€18,000€60,945
6540 years€24,000€131,474

The key insight: It is not about how much you invest. It is about how long you invest. Time in the market beats timing the market every single time.

The real barrier is psychology, not money

€50 per month feels too small to matter. But that feeling is the enemy of wealth. The most important step is building the habit. Automate your savings, forget about them and let compound interest work quietly in the background.
